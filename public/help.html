Help & Guide - Secret Slices

Learn how to securely split and recover secrets using Shamir's Secret Sharing.

Help & Guide

This application utilizes Shamir's Secret Sharing, a cryptographic method for splitting a secret into multiple shares. It ensures that the secret can only be recovered when a predefined number of shares are combined, enhancing security and privacy.

To begin using Secret Slices, simply navigate to SecretSlices.com. No initial setup is required.

To split a secret, enter the secret message, threshold, and number of shares. Click "Split Secret" to generate the shares. You can then download the shares or copy into the recovery form to test recovery.

To recover a secret, click on the necessary number of parts to add it to the recover form & click "Recover Secret". The secret will be displayed if the threshold is met.

Security Recommendations

When sharing and storing shares, it's crucial to use secure methods. Consider the environment and the trust level of participants when setting the threshold for recovery.

About

SecretSlices.com is created for educational purposes and to provide a simple, secure method for splitting and recovering secrets. It is not intended for use in high-risk environments or for critical data.
